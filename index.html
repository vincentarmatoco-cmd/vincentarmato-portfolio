About

Athlete and Photographer

My name is Vincent Armato. I'm a first-year Supply Chain Management student at the University of Tennessee.

I've spent most of my life as an athlete, playing football and basketball for all 4 years of High School. That background is what pulled me into sports photography and videography. As the primary media contractor for St. Charles East High School, I've covered media days for nearly every team, along with state championships for various sports at other high schools. I've also worked with collegiate and professional teams, including the Windy City Bulls and NIU Huskies.

Alongside that, I run my own video production agency, BigDog Media, where I help local businesses grow their social media through video content that converts.

Being an athlete gives me a skill set most sports creatives don't have. I understand the game from the inside, which helps me capture moments and see them from the athlete's point of view.

Outside of work, I stay active by being outside and doing things like hiking, wakeboarding, snowboarding, and playing pickleball. I also really enjoy spending time with my friends and family.
